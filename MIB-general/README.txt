General board for MIB projects. MIDI in (via 6N137 optocoupler)
and out (via 74LS14 buffer), SVG pin groups for easy connection
of potentiometers, switches and LEDs to A0-A3 and digital pins
10, 11 and 12. Optional battery power supply part with soldered
in charge/step-up board.

Component list:

1x Arduino Pro Mini
1x 6N137 optocoupler, DIP
1x 74LS14 buffer/inverter, DIP
1x 1N4148 diode
1x 10K resistor
3x 220 ohm resistor
2x PCB mount DIN-5 MIDI PCB connector
1x Slide switch DPDT (2.54 mm pin separation)
Breakable pin headers, black, red, yellow

Optional:

Battery holder 16340
Li-Ion battery 3.7V 16340
All-in-one charge and step-up board (r.ebay.com-uWUNOm)
